I often get asked how these images were made, so I made this site to provide some explanations.

They are drawn by hand using mostly colored pens, then scanned and filled using GIMP. I especially like the "dissolve" and "burn" fill effects. Mouseover the images below to see what I mean.

Then one day, I realized it would be possible to improve on this process by automating the tracing of contours. I'm in IT, and that's just what we do. To this end, I've created the Doodler. Some of the images below have no original paper form because they were created using the Doodler.

If you're interested in owning a large print of one of these images, please contact me and let me know. Most sizes are available for less than 30 Euros.
